Barça, is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain, that competes in La Liga, the top flight of Spanish football.
Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, Catalan, German, and English footballers led by Joan Gamper, the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture and Catalanism, hence the motto "Més que un club" ("More than a club")
Domestically, Barcelona has won a record ,

  • 76 trophies
  • 26 La Liga
  • 31 Copa del Rey
  • 14 Supercopa de España,
  • 3 Copa Eva Duarte
  • 2 Copa de la Liga titles
as well as being the record holder for the latter four competitions.
In international club football, the club has won, 17 European and worldwide titles:
  • 5 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 4 UEFA Cup Winners' Cups
  • 5 UEFA Super Cups
  • 3 FIFA Club World Cups
